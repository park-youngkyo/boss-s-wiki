### 🎯 **The Single Most Valuable Next Action**

**TASK:** **Develop the Core Product Content Map and MVP Outline.**

**GOAL:** To translate the high-level marketing strategy (Funnel/Persona) into a tangible, sequential, and measurable content pipeline that represents the Minimum Viable Product (MVP) of the AI-powered service.

**WHY THIS IS CRITICAL:** This task forces the synthesis of all previous discussions. It moves the project from the conceptual whiteboard to the actionable content calendar, providing immediate, testable deliverables.

### 🛠️ **Execution Plan & Required Deliverables**

This task should be executed by a **Content Strategy Lead** role, synthesizing knowledge from all prior inputs. The output must be structured in the following three parts:

#### **1. The Funnel-to-Content Mapping (The "What")**
*   **Deliverable:** A table or matrix mapping the entire customer journey (Awareness $\rightarrow$ Consideration $\rightarrow$ Decision $\rightarrow$ Retention).
*   **Detail:** For each stage, specify:
    *   The *Pain Point* addressed.
    *   The *Goal* of the content at this stage.
    *   The *Format* (Blog, Video Script, Lead Magnet, Interactive Tool).
    *   The *Success Metric* (e.g., Sign-up Rate, Time on Page, Demo Request).

#### **2. The MVP Content Deep Dive (The "First 3 Pieces")**
*   **Deliverable:** Detailed outlines for the **first three pieces of content** that must be created to kickstart the MVP (e.g., 1 piece for Awareness, 1 for Consideration, 1 for Decision).
*   **Detail:** For each piece:
    *   **Title/Topic:** Must be highly clickable and benefit-driven.
    *   **Outline:** A detailed section-by-section structure (H1, H2, H3 points).
    *   **AI Prompting Strategy:** Include 3-5 specific, advanced prompts that the AI engine would use to generate the draft content, ensuring the tone and required expertise are maintained.

#### **3. Resource Allocation & Feedback Loop (The "How to Improve")**
*   **Deliverable:** A short section outlining the next iteration step.
*   **Detail:** Based on the MVP launch, what is the *single most likely failure point* or *bottleneck*? And what is the immediate, low-effort test to validate the assumption before investing in the next major feature?
