#### 📋 What We Need to Produce: The Pillar Blueprint

This blueprint must contain the following sections:

1.  **The Pillar Topic Confirmation:** (e.g., "The Definitive Guide to X for Y Industry").

5.  **SEO Directives:** Placement notes for internal linking, schema markup suggestions, and recommended Call-to-Action placements within the text.
6.  **Design & Visual Directives:** A list of required assets (e.g., "Need an infographic comparing X vs Y," "Must include a workflow diagram").
